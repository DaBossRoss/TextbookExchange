OUR SERVICES

JMU Textbook Exchange provides a platform for all students of James Madison University to conveniently purchase and sell their used textbooks directly from each other instead of having to go through the bookstore. By having student to student to student transactions, this cuts out the middle man and allows buys to pay at a reduced cost as well as buyers receiving more money for their products.

Students will be able to contact each other and set up an appointment to meet at their own convenience. JMU Textbook Exchange a platform for student access and does not guarantee that all textbooks for classes with be available. We are not liable for damaged books, if you get robbed, kidnapped, raped, shot, or if a relationship doesn't work out between your buyer/sell. Please pick a safe place (preferably on campus) where there are people around.
